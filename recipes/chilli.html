Texas red cowboy chilli

This deliciously meaty dish features three authentic mexican chilli's toasted and steeped in water to rehydrate them before blending into a lucious red chilli paste.

Ingredients

  • 2 Large onions, diced
  • 2 KG chuck steak
  • 3 Teaspoons vegetable oil
  • Half cup of chilli powder
  • 3 Tablespoons of cumin powder
  • 1 Tablespoon of dried oregano
  • 6 Cloves of garlic, peeled and minced
  • 1 Tin of chopped tomatos
  • 2 Beef stock cubes or 600ML of homemade stock
  • 2 Limes
